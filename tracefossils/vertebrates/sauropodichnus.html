Sauropodichnus Calvo, 1991


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Manus tracks are crescent shape and are wider than long (40 cm by 25 cm) but are smaller than pes tracks. Anterior end of manus has a convex border while posterior end has a concave border. Wide trackway. Pes tracks have a lateral diameter length between 60 to 100 cm. Pes track is circular when poorly preserved and subtriangular when poorly preserved. Pace angulation is greater than 60 degrees.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Sauropod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Calvo, 1999
