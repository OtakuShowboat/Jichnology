Picunichnus Calvo, 1991


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tetradactyl track with distinct claw impressions. Digit I is medially directed, relatively small and is on the posterior side of the hell. Digit III is the longest digit.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapoda

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

REMARKS: Information taken from Calvo, 1999
