Dilophosauripus Welles, 1971


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl track with digit II being short, thick and offset. Digits III and IV are nearly equal in length and digit II is shorter. Angle of divarication between digits II=IV is 54 degrees. Extremely long claws. No separate metatarsal pad mark. Stride length is about three times the length of the pes.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapod, likely Dilophosaurus

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Triassic
