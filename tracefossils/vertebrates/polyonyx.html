--- layout: traces title: Polyonyx ---

Polyonyx Santos et al., 2009


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Wide trackway with manus and pes impressions. Manus has a large digit I impression and is assymetrical. This digit is medially directed and has a large, triangular claw impression that is posteriorly oriented. Digits II-V are slightly bent. Pes impression with claws I and II facing anteriorly and III and IV facing laterally.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Sauropod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Middle Jurassic
