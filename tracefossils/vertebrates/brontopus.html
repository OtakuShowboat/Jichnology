Brontopus Heyler and Lessertisseur, 1963


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Trackway of a quadruped that is semiplantigrade to plantigrade and has pentadactyl manus and pedes. The manus and pes imprints are of greater width than length and well defined claw impressions are present. GEOLOGIC RANGE: Permian
