Praehominipes Medrum et al., 2011


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Plantigrade, entaxonic track that is elongated. Only pes impressions are present. Lacks a longitudinal arch. Heel is tapered and digits II-V extend distally relative to the hallux (digit I).

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Early hominids

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Pliocene
