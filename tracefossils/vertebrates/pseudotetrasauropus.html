Pseudotetrasauropus Ellenberger 1972


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Digitgrade pentadactyl track with the first four digits facing anteriorly and the fifth one located in the rear. Digits are not connected to each other. Foot axis varies in relation to trackway.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Unknown, foot structure of prosauropods fit the morphology of the track the best.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Triassic
