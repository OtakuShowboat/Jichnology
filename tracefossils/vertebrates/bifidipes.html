--- layout: traces title: Bifidipes ---

Bifidipes Demathieu et al., 1984


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Didactyl track composed of two individual digits. The digits are in the shape of hoofprints. The digits are separated and pointed and the slight depression at the rear is not always distinct. The tracks are longer than they are wide.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Artiodactyla

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Pleistocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended in Fornos et al., 2002. Fanelli et al., 2007
