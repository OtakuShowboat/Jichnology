Parabaropus Lull 1918


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): Magapezia coloradensis

DESCRIPTION: Quadrupedal, plantigrade tracks, five digits on both manus and pes, manus is smaller than pes, digis in both relatively short and have rounded ends, no claws present, sole of pes elongated and narrows towards the back, manus has strong inward turning and is placed in front of the pes.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Reptillia

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Permian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Gilmore, 1927. Hunt and Lucas, 2005
