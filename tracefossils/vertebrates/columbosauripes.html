Columbosauripes Steinberg, 1932


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small, semi-digitigrade, tridactyl track. Digits II–IV have a wide angle of divarication. Digits II and IV are equal to nearly equal in length and width. Phalangeal pads are restricted to only digit III. Claw impressions are long. Digit II–III has a wider angle of divarication than digits III–IV. All digits are connected to the metatarsal pad. The heel is broad and is around to amorphous.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: A coelurosaur, ornithomimid, or a mid-sized therapod.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Gangloff et al., 2004
