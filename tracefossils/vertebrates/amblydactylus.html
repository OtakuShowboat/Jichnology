Amblydactylus Sternberg, 1932


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tridactl track that is as wide or wider than it is long. Digits II and IV diverge at a small angle relative to the pedal axis. Metatarsal-phalangeal pad is posteriorly located and is distinct. Interdigital webbing connects the proximal end of the digits together. Digits end in blunt claws. Narrow and long trackway.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Ornithopod dinosaur

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Curries and Sarjeant, 1979
