Characichnos Whyte and Romano, 2001


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Swim tracks that consist of elongate, parallel to sub-parallel ridges or “scrape marks” that occur almost consistantly without walking traces. Ridges may be straight, gently curved or slightly sinuous.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Aquatic

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Dinosaurs, crocodilians, chelonians and amphibians

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Jurassic
