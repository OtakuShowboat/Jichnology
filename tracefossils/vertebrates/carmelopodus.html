Carmelopodus Lockley et al., 1998


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Subsymmetrical tridactyl track. For digits II–III–IV, the digital pad formula is 2–3–3. Claw impressions apparent. Tracks are nearly as wide as they are long. Digits’ angle of divarication is high especially between II–III. The trackway is narrow and digit III is parallel to the trackway axis.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapoda dinosaurs.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Triassic–Middle Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Gierlinski et al., 2009
