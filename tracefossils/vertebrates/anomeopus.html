Anomoepus Hitchcock, 1848


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): Hopiichnus, Moyenisauropus

DESCRIPTION: Small tetradactyl pes tracks that are more often tridactyl, manus tracks sometimes are preserved. Digit IVâ€™s metatarsal-phalangeal pad is almost directly in line, or is, with the axis of digit III. Digit I is relatively long. In the manus, digit III is the longest with the other digits decreasing in size going away from digit III. Tail marks can be seen in sitting traces.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Primivite members of ornithischian dinosaurs including Fabrosaurs, Thyreophora and probably Marginocephalia and Ornithopoda.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Jurassic

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Olsen and Rainforth, 2003
