Anthichnium Haubold, 1971


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small, sprawling quadruped tracks. Prints are generally indistinct, but the overall pattern of sinuous movement is clear. Individual prints are generally 10 mm or less in length; the pes having longer digits than the manus; four manus digits and five pes digits; and a plantigrade track.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Small sprawling quadruped; small temnospondyl amphibian with four manus digits and five pes digits.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Carboniferous-Permian
