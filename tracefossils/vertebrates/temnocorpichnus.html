--- layout: traces title: Temnocorpichnus ---

Temnocorpichnus Lucus et al., 2010


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Body impression with a smooth integument, triangular head impression, four robust limbs with Batrachichnus tracks, relatively short trunk and long, tapering tail. It is important to note that Batrachichnus tracks are a distinct component of this trace making Temnocorpichnus a compound ichnofossil.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Temnospondyl amphibian

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Mississippian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Description from Lucas, Fillmore, and Simpson, 2013
