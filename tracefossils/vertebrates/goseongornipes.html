Goseongornipes Lockley et al., 2006


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A somewhat semipalmated tetradactyl track with wide angle of divarication between digits II–IV. Digit I is small and points posteriorly. Webbing restricted between digits II–IV.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environment

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Shorebirds

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous
