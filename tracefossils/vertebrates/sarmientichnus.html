Sarmientichnus Casamiquela, 1964


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): Casamiquelichnus

DESCRIPTION: Didactyl tracks with digit I oriented caudally and digit III oriented cranially. Both digits are directly inline with each other and are usually indistinguishable from each other.
