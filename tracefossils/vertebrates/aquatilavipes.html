--- layout: traces title: Aquatilavipes ---

Aquatilavipes Currie, 1981


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: An incumbant anisodactyl track, divarication angle between digits II–IV are greater than 100 degrees. Apparent claw impressions on each digit. Digit IV is shorter than digit III and longer than digit II creating an asymmetrical appearance. Width of the track is greater than its length. Digit III has three to four faint pad impressions.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environments.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Shoreline birds.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cretaceous-Eocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Lockley et al., 1992; Fiorillo et al., 2010
