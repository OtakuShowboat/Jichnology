DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl tracks with thick, U-shaped digits. Angle of divarication between digits II-IV is from 65 to 70 degrees. Trackway is narrow and the pace angulation is about 170 degrees. Tracks are positively rotated inward. Stride length to track length ratio is from 4.1 to 4.6. Trackway width goes up to 69 cm and is about 1.6 times the trackâ€™s width. Manus impressions may also be present.
