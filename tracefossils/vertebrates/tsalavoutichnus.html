PENDING REVIEW: Tsalavoutichnus Hogue and Hasiotis, in review


DESCRIPTION: Sinuous to slightly sinuous series of discontinuous to continuous impressions (epirelief) or mounds (hyporelief). Impressions may overlap within a single trail. Usually associated with bird tracks.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia, Praedichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environment

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Shorebirds

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Eocene
