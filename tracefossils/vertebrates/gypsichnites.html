Gypsichnites Steinberg, 1932


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl track with a broadly rounded heel. Track is short and squat, toes are thick and are partially connected by a web. Digits terminated bluntly.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Iguanodontids

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous
