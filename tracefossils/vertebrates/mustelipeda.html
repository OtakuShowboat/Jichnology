Mustelipeda Kordos, 1985


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small, circular track with five digits closely connected to it.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Mustelid animals

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Miocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Description is the same as the ichnospecies, Mustelipeda punctate. Photo of track is not included in Kordos, 1985.
