Haenamichnus Hwang, 2002


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Consists of very large manus and pes. Impressions are digitigrade, tridactyl, stongly asymmetric and lacking claw impressions. Digit III is longer and more deeply impressed than digits I and II; digit III being substantially longer than digit II. Show no sign of the 5th phlange of the pes. The toes are triangular or rounded in shape distally without distinct claw impressions.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Pterosaurs (possible Pteranodon and azhdarchids)

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Cretaceous
