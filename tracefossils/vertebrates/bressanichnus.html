Bressanichnus Calvo, 1991


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small tridactyl tracks with noticiable claw impressions. Digit III curves interiorly. The posterior end of the track is rounded and small.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapods

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

REMARKS: Information taken from Calvo, 1999.
