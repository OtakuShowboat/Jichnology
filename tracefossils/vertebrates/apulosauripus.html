Apulosauripus Nicosia et al., 1999


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Trackway of a quadrupedal dinosaurian with tridactyl manus and pes. The size of the manus is less than one half the pes. The manus impressions are anterior and medial to the pes impressions. (4:1 stride to foot length) The pace-angulation of this trackway is very low.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Ornithopod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cretaceous
