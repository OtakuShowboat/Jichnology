Tayassuichnum Remeika, 2006


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: A peccary-like (new world pig) track.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Artiodactyla animals, probably made by an extinct peccary.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Pleistocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: The lack of an image for the holotype and an adequate description of the ichnogenus combined with the ichnogenus not published in a peer-reviewed paper makes this ichnogenus validity questionable. We therefore declare this Tayassucihnum nomen nudum.
