Iguanodontipus Sarjeant et al., 1998


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Trackway of a bipedal dinosaurian with tridactyl and semidigitigrade pedes. All three digits are similar in length. Digit II and IV are laterally directed while digit III is directed towards the midline. The right and left pedes imprints are closely spaced but the stride length is relatively long.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Iguanadontid

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Jurassic–Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Meyer and Thuring, 2003; Gierliński et al., 2009
