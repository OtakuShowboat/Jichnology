Dinehichnus Lockley et al., 1998


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small to medium-sized, symmetrical, tridactyl tracks that are almost as wide as they are long. Distinct, circular heel pad impression. Digits are separate from the heel pad. Digits are elliptical with blunt claw impressions. Angle of divarication between digits II–IV are about 90 degrees. Narrow trackway with tride length about three times the track length. Digit III rotated 10–15 degrees inward relative to trackway.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Ornithopod dinosaurs

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Jurassic
