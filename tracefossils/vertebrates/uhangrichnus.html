--- layout: traces title: Uhangrichnus ---

Uhangrichnus Yang et al., 1995


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Palmated, incumbent anisodactyl tracks. Tracks are wider than long. The angle of divarication between digits II–IV is about 100 degrees. Digits II and IV curve slightly inward. The anterior end of the webbing concaves slightly anteriorly between digits II–III and III–IV.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Water-margin environment

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Plover-like birds (Charadriiformes), or Anseriform-like birds.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Cretaceous–Eocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Mansilla, 2012
