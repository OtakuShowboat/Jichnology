--- layout: traces title: Corpulentapus ---

Corpulentapus Li et al., 2011


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Robust tridactyl track with low angle of divarication among digits and weak mesaxony. Has a ‘fluer de lys’ shape. Trak is nearly as wide as it is long. Digits II–IV are thick. For most of the digit traces’ proximal length, a hypex does not separate them. Trackway is long and narrow.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Dinosaurian, either therapoda or ornithopoda.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cretaceous
