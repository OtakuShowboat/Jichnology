Asianopodus Matsukawa et al., 2005


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Medium to small subsymetrical, tridactlyl track. Heel impression is apparent and bulbous. The track impression is longer than wide. Angle of divarication among digits is quite narrow.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapoda dinosaurs

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Xing et al., 2011
