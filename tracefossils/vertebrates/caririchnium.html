Caririchnium Leonardi, 1984


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Trackway with manus and pes impressions. In quadrupedal trackway, manus is small and elongated and pes is large, plantigrade and tridactyl. In the bipedal trackway, the tracks are large and are tridactyl and the trackway is wide. The quadrupedal trackway’s pace angulation is higher than the bipedal’s. Manus and pes impressions have negative rotation.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Ornithopod dinosaurs, likely hadrosaurids or iguanodontids

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Lee, 1997.
