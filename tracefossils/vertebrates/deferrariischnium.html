Deferrariischnium Calvo, 1991


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl track with digit III comprising more than fifty percent of the length of the track and ending in a distinct claw trace. Digits II and IV are small and can be hard to notice as well as their claw impressions. Posterior end of the track is acute.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Small therapod dinosaur

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Information taken from Calvo, 1999
