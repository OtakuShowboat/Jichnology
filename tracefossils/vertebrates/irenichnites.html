Irenichnites Sternberg, 1932


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Tridactyl track with all digits similar in thickness. Digit II separate from the track. Phalangeal pads can be faintly seen. Stride length is relatively long and the trackway is narrow.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Therapod possibly ornithomimisaurian

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Lockley et a., 2011
