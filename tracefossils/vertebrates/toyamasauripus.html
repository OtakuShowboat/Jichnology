Toyamasauripus Matsukawa et al., 1997


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Small tridactyl track (3-10 cm long) that has a narrow trackway and a high pace angulation. Digits are slender with pad impressions sometimes preserved. The anterior end of digit III curves inward. Track is slightly asymmetrical and width and length are nearly equal. Angle of divarication between digits II-IV are about 90 degrees.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Either a therapod or ornithopod dinosaur.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Lower Cretaceous
