--- layout: traces title: Bijugopeda ---

Bijugopeda Sarjeant and Reynolds, 1999


JUNIOR SYNONYM(S): N/A

DESCRIPTION: Roundish, digitigrade, bidigital tracks. Across the anterior-posterior axis and the lateral axis, the two digits are roughly symmetrical. Large interdigital trough with a large medial pocket. Claw impressions poorly preserved or absent.

BEHAVIOR(S): Repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental; terrestrial

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Artiodactyla, likely camelid taxa.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Mid-miocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: McNeil et al., 2007
