Pilichnia Chamberlain, 1971


DESCRIPTION: Vertical to horizontal, subcylindrical to ovoid burrow up to 6 cm in diameter.

BEHAVIOR(S): Domichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Organism burrowed through sand to underlying shale

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: May be holothurians or arthropods

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Mississippian-Pennsylvanian
