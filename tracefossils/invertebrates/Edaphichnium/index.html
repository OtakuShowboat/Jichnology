Edaphichnium Bown and Kraus, 1983


DESCRIPTION: Elongate traces filled with randomly oriented pellets. Range in size from less than 0.5 cm to 2 cm in diameter. May be over 10 cm in length.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion, habitation, and deposit feeding. The visible pellets on the trace are remnants of feces.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Various continental settings, particularly within well-developed soils.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Earthworms

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Hasiotis, 2006)
