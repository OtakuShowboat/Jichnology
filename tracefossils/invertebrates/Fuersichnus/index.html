Fuersichnus Bromely and Asgaard, 1979


DESCRIPTION: U-shaped tubes that are comprised of convex outward retrusive spreite. alls may be smooth or bulbous. Typically 0.3-1 cm in diameter and 0.1-2 cm in depth.

BEHAVIOR(S): Deposit feeding

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Terrestrial environments. Indicates a close proximity to a water source, but not completely submerged. Found near floodplains or ephemeral streams.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Mayfly larvae

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Hasiotis, 2002, 2004, 2006
