Halopoa Torell, 1870


DESCRIPTION: Long, horizontal burrows with irregular longitudinal ridges or wrinkles

BEHAVIOR(S): Feeding burrow of a deposit-feeding organism

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Found in sandstone turbidites in deep marine deposits
