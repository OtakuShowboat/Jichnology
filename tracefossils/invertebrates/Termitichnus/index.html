--- layout: traces title: Termitichnus ---

Termitichnus Smith et al., 1993


DESCRIPTION: Mosaic patterns of networks of simple and complex galleries, chambers, and ramps.

BEHAVIOR(S): Multiple use nest. Includes feeding, reproduction, storage, and growth fungi for food (Hasiotis, 2003).

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Various continental settings, but specifically in the upper vadose zone of soils. Typically occur in proximal to distal alluvial settings and near lacustrine settings. Have been reported in eolian environments (Seely and Mitchell, 1986; Loope, 2008).

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Termites

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent.

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Hasiotis and Dubiel, 1995
