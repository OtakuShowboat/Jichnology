Augerinoichnus Minter et al., 2008


DESCRIPTION: Linear succession of horseshoe-shaped structures on the bedding plane.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Alluvial plain and tidal flat

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Vermiform animal

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Permian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Reconstructed as an infaunal, horizontal, helical-shaped burrow.
