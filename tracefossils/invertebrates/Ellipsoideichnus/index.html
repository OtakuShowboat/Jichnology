Ellipsoideichnus Roselli, 1933?


DESCRIPTION: Arrangement of Celliforma-like cells attached to tunnels. Helicoidal tunnel with two opposite cells per whorl.

BEHAVIOR(S): Reproduction, dwelling.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Continental. Found in proximity to aquatic environments but above the watertable.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Bees.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent.

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Genise, 2000; Hasiotis, 2003
