Syntermesichnus Bown and Laza, 1990


DESCRIPTION: Nest structure consisting of multiple chambers and galleries.

BEHAVIOR(S): Multiple use colonial nest.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Fully continental

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Termites

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Hasiotis, 2003)

REMARKS: Differs from other subterranean termite nests in its architectural organization.
