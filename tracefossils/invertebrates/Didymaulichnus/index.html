Didymaulichnus Young, 1972


DESCRIPTION: Gently curving, smooth, surficial trail or burrow that is bisected longitudinally by a median furrow.

BEHAVIOR(S): Pascichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Turbidites; shallow marine

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Gastropod, molluscs, and arthropod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Precambrian-Cretaceous

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Shawna, 1989; Bhawanisingh 2010
