Wasp Cocoons


DESCRIPTION: Small, ovoid capsules.

BEHAVIOR(S): Reproduction. Similar to Celliforma, these are created to house eggs and hold food for insect larvae.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Range of continental settings, occur above the water table in both proximal and distal alluvial and lacustrine settings.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Wasps.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Triassic-recent.
