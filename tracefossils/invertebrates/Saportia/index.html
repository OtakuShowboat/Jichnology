Saportia Squinabol 1891


JR SYN: Haentzschelinia, Saportia

DESCRIPTION: Large tunnel passageways, commonly branching dichotomously, surface with rhombic pattern produced by delicately arched parallel striations in 2 systems.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia, repichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Marine, flysch deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: N/A
