Oldhamia Forbes, 1849


DESCRIPTION: Horizontal burrow of fine rills radiating from joints of sympodial axis

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia and pasichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep marine, rarely shallow marine

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: undermat miners (Seilacher et al. 2005)

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: White et al. (2012)

REMARKS: Description from Hantzschel (1975). Age range is loosely constrained
