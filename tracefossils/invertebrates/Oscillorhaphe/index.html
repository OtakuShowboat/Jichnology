Oscillorhaphe Seilacher, 1977


DESCRIPTION: Hypichnial strings arranged in sharply turning meanders. Meanders have a distinct crossbar.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia, pasichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Fully marine, trace occurs in deep marine flysch deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: unknown

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Paleocene-Eocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Uchman, 1998
