Cladichnus Heer, 1877


DESCRIPTION: Ringlike to beadlike burrow fills forming meniscus segments; smooth walled burrows are branched and radiating; wall lining is very thin or not present.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep-water turbidites; flysch deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Unknown

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cretaceous-Eocene
