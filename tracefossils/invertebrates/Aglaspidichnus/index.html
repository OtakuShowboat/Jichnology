Aglaspidichnus Radwanski and Roniewicz, 1967


DESCRIPTION:Hieroglyph trace on the bedding plane with an axial ridge with eight branches curving rearwards from the central axis; all branches are spaces relatively equidistant from one another.

BEHAVIOR(S): Cubichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Shallow marine

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Arthropod; Xiphosura

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cambrian
