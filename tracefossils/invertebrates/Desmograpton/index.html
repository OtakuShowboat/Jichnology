Desmograpton Fuchs, 1895


DESCRIPTION: Straight or undulating burrow with lateral appendages; smooth convex hyporelief; burrow diameter 0.3-1.7 mm

BEHAVIOR(S): Pascichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep-water turbidity environments

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Polychaete worms

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Silurian-Miocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Uchman, 1998; McCann, 1989

REMARKS: Description from McCann (1989).
