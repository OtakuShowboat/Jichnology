Phycosiphon (Fischer-Ooster, 1858)


DESCRIPTION: Extensive small-scale spreiten-rich trace composed of repeated narrow, U-shaped lobes extending from a central spreiten. Lobes are parallel or oblique to bedding.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia; deposit-feeding.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Fully marine.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Veriform organism.

GEOLOGIC RANGE: ?Ordovician - ?recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: (Emended: Wetzel and Bromley, 1994; Uchman ,1998)(Crimes, 1992).
