Climactichnites Logan, 1860


DESCRIPTION: A trail or burrow of undulating bars and furrows oriented perpendicular or at an angle; variable widths, usually between 2 and 18 cm, but can reach 30 cm.

BEHAVIOR(S): Locomotion

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Shallow tide- and wave-influenced marine; intertidal zone.

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Mollusc; gastropod

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Upper Cambrian

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Emended by Yochelson and Fedonkin, 1993 and Getty and Hagadorn, 2008. Getty and Hagadorn 2009.

REMARKS: Description from Getty and Hagadorn (2008, 2009).
