Capodistria Vialov 1968


DESCRIPTION: Radiating ridges from an oval or circular area; ridges are short and smoothed walled; knobs or a series of knobs are located in the center.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep-water turbidites; flysch deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: N/A

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Cretaceous - Miocene

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Uchman, 1998

REMARKS: Description from Uchman (1998)
