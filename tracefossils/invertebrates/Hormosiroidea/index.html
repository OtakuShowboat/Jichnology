Hormosiroidea Schaffer, 1928


DESCRIPTION: Subspherical, angular, semisphereical chamber-like bodies joined by horizontal strings; strings can emerge perpendicular from the spheres.

BEHAVIOR(S): Calichnia

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep-water turbidites; flysch deposits

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: N/A

GEOLOGIC RANGE: N/A

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES: Seilacher, 1977; Uchman, 1995 and 1998

REMARKS: Description from Uchman (1998)
