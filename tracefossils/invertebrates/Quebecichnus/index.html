Quebecichnus Hofmann, 1972


DESCRIPTION: Uniformly branching, rectolinear tunnels developed along bedding planes commonly filled with cylindroidal to ellipsoidal pellets.

BEHAVIOR(S): Fodinichnia; dwelling and feeding burrows of deposit feeders.

ENVIRONMENTAL SETTINGS: Deep marine settings

POSSIBLE TRACEMAKERS: Unknown

GEOLOGIC RANGE: Ordovician - ?recent

ADDITIONAL REFERENCES:

REMARKS: Thalassinoides variant
